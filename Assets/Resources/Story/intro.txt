Welcome to Project Gamelan. 
This is a blended music and story experience brought to you by Wai Kei and Sapphire. 
Through this game, we hope that you'll learn more about the music of the gamelan, as well as the themes of unity and harmonny that such music can convey. 
You may progress through a series of nodes through the map. 
Every node will either contain a musical challenge, or a slice-of-life story cutscene. 
We hope that this will be a fun, perhaps relaxing, perhaps entertaining game.
